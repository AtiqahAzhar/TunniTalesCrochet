To place an order with us, you must be over 18 years of age and possess a valid credit or debit card issued by a bank acceptable to us.

Available Payment Methods
We accept payments through TikTok Shop, Instagram DM, WhatsApp, Shopee, and bank transfers. Specific payment instructions will be provided during checkout or upon order confirmation.
